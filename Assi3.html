About


Running Man is a South Korean variety show, forming part of SBS' Good Sunday lineup. It was first aired on July 11, 2010. Running Man was originally classified as an "urban action variety"; a genre of variety shows in an urban environment. The MCs and guests were to complete missions at a landmark to win the race.
